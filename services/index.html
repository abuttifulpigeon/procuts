FAQ

Can I book an appointment?

Procuts is a walk-in service. Appointments required for chemical services and updos only.

Can I leave and come back if there's a wait?

Procuts is a first-come first-serve solon. If you choose to leave during a long wait, and your name is called and you are not present, your name will be removed from the list.

Please be aware wait times are an estimate.
